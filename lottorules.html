Texas Lone Star Lotto Rules

How to Play

  1. Select 6 numbers from 1 to 54.
  2. Click Draw the Lone Star Win! to see if you match the winning numbers.
  3. If you match at least 3 numbers, you win a prize!

Game Notes

  • Jackpot prize is an average and may vary for each drawing.
  • Prizes for 5, 4, and 3 matches are set prizes, but may be split if there are multiple winners.
  • All prizes are for demonstration only in this game.
  • You must match at least 3 numbers to win.
  • For official rules, visit the Texas Lottery website.
